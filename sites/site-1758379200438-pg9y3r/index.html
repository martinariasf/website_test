Caring for Black Clothes

01

Wash Inside Out

Prevents fading and protects the fabric

02

Cold Water Only

Maintains color intensity and prevents shrinking

03

Separate Darks

Avoid lint transfer from lighter fabrics

04

Air Dry When Possible

Reduces fading and extends garment life
