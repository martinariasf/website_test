How to Keep Your White Shoes Pristine

1. Regular Cleaning

Clean your white shoes weekly with a soft brush and mild soap solution.

2. Protective Spray

Apply water and stain repellent spray before first wear and regularly after.

3. Proper Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent yellowing.

4. Quick Stain Removal

Address stains immediately using appropriate cleaning methods for the material.
