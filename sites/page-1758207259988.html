Fun Facts About 15

  • 15 is the sum of the first 5 positive integers (1+2+3+4+5)
  • It's a triangular number and a composite number
  • 15 minutes = quarter of an hour
  • In Roman numerals, 15 is written as XV
  • 15 is the atomic number of phosphorus
